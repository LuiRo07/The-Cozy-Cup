Mission Statement

We Believe A Good Day Starts With A Good Cup of Coffee

Caffe Latte

A Rich, full-bodied espresso in steamed milk, lightly topped with foam with a shot or two of bold, tasty espresso with fresh, steamed milk over it.

Banana Iced Latte

An ice-cold blended latte made up of a shot of espreso, cold milk, and fresh bananas.

Matcha Latte

A sweet, morish vegetal taste latte made from finely ground tea leaves mixed with warm steamed milk.

Mocha Latte

A Hot and sweet combination of steamed milk, espresso and chocolate syrup, a perfect choice for chocolate lovers!

Spanish Latte

A unique latte from Spain, that consists of one part espresso, one part milk, and one part condensed milk, garnished with fresh ground cinnamon.
